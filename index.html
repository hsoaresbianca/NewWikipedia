Game of Thrones

Overview

Game of Thrones is a fantasy drama television series based on the novel series A Song of Ice and Fire by George R. R. Martin. The show first premiered on HBO in 2011 and concluded in 2019.

Plot

The story takes place in the fictional continents of Westeros and Essos, where noble families fight for control of the Iron Throne. The series is known for its complex characters, intricate plotlines, and shocking twists.

Characters

  • Jon Snow
  • Daenerys Targaryen
  • Tyrion Lannister
  • Cersei Lannister
  • Sansa Stark
  • Arya Stark
